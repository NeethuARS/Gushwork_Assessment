What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminum tubing is lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and highly durable, making it ideal for applications requiring strength without added weight.

How is aluminum tubing produced?

It is typically produced through extrusion or drawing processes, where aluminum is shaped into tubes with precise dimensions and thickness.
